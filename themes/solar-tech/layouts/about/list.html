Group Background

Ningbo Solar Tech Import & Export Co., Ltd. is a wholly-ownedsubsidiary of Winpex Group (one of the largest export enterprises in Ningbo). Ningbo Winpex group was established in 2003, with a registered capital of 100 million yuan, with a 20,000-square-meter office space and a 6,000-square-meter specialized product display center. It owns 8 subsidiaries and has branches in the United States and the United Kingdom. The core business of Winpex is exporting the daily necessities. At same time, Winpex expands the related field, optimizes product structure, and enhances service capabilities. Winpex has developed into a set of a diversified industrial cluster and focus on 'self-operated export, foreign trade incubation, manufacturing, cross-border e-commerce, and supply chain management'.

Winpex takes "fight for the dream" as our core values, and always adheres to the spirit of "pursuing excellence, undertaking responsibilities and staying positive ". We value the acquisition and the training of talents and have built a transparent promotion system. Winpex encourages “healthy culture” and “building strong body and mind”. We always maintain industry-leading professional capabilities, customer reputation, innovation efficiency, and performance levels, and pursue top market positions and competitive strengths.
